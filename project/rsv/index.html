RSV vaccine modelling

 

RSV is one of the main viral causes of pneumonia in young children. There is no licenced vaccine against RSV but there are some which are under development. In this project, we aim to develop an RSV mathematical model to investigate the various vaccination strategies for its control in both developing and developed country settings. We also aim to identify important vaccine characteristics.
